The NEOM

NEOM is a new destination being created in the Northwest of Saudi Arabia, along the Red Sea and Gulf of Aqaba with principles of livability and innovation at its core. It will be the home to future industries and technologies and the best place to live and work. NEOM is fully owned and financially backed by the Public Investment Fund of Saudi Arabia.
